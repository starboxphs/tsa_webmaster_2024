Energy-Efficient Appliances

Basic information: Energy-efficient appliances are designed to optimize energy use without sacrificing performance or comfort. They often incorporate advanced technologies and engineering innovations that improve efficiency while maintaining high-quality performance1. These appliances are a smart choice for both your wallet and the environment.

Cost: The cost of new appliances varies based on the type and features. Provided below are a range of costs depending on what you want to install: Refrigerators: $400 to $3,000+ each. Dishwashers: Varying prices depending on the model and features. HVAC Systems: These account for 40% of your energy costs.

Tax rebate information: Up to $3,200 after January 1, 2023, and through 2032.

Practices: While shopping for a appliance, look for the EPA Energy Star Label, as these tends to provide the best energy efficienty within your homes. Also, be sure to opt for models with features like smart sensors, variable speed settings, and energy-saving modes.
