Green energy home initiatives

This websites creation is intended to inform homeowners on how they can make changes to their home and lives in order to reduce their carbon footprint. By providing this information that includes tax rebates, financial benefits, and general information to encourage them to make the smart choice

Why is it important to reduce carbon footprint?

Climate change is rapid increase of global average temperatures that is threatening species, food security, and human safety through the various consequences such as a rise in sea levels, extreme doubts and storms, and unfamiliar climate that crops and animals are not use to. Climate change is being caused by human activity. The human activity most responsible is the use of carbon fuels to supply the demanding power grid. The electricity on the grid is used to power and heat our home. By reducing energy consumption or switching to carbon neutral renewable energy, homeowners can reap the benefits of a low power bill and help fight the malignant threat of climate change.
